Home → Family

One budget for the household. No shared passwords.

Most couples manage money across two phones, three apps and a spreadsheet nobody updates. Gilt gives both of you the same live ledger — while each of you keeps your own Apple account, your own Face ID, your own phone.

How Family works

Shared household ledger

Invite your partner from the app. The ledger syncs through your own iCloud accounts via Apple's CloudKit sharing — no Gilt server in the middle, ever.

One subscription for up to 5

The Family plan uses Apple Family Sharing: buy once, and members of your Apple family group are unlocked automatically on their own devices.

Everyone edits, everyone sees

Both partners add transactions, manage budgets and see reports — one clear, always-current picture of the household money.

Built for real households

  • Joint groceries and rent next to personal accounts — all in one place
  • Multi-currency households handled properly (salary in one currency, spending in another)
  • Recurring bills visible to both — no more "wait, what charged us €40?"
  • Each person signs in with their own Apple ID — nothing to share, nothing to leak

Privacy, still

Family Sharing extends the subscription; iCloud sharing extends the data — between your own Apple accounts only. Gilt still has no servers and still can't see a single transaction. How that works →
