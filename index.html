Frequently asked

Where is my data stored? On your device and in your personal iCloud (CloudKit private database). We can't see it — there is nothing on our side to see it with.

Do I need an account? No. Gilt uses the iCloud account already on your iPhone.

Is it really free? The full tracker is free: accounts, budgets, reports, recurring payments, widgets, iCloud sync.
