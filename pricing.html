The data promise

Your data is never held hostage. If your trial ends or you cancel, Gilt switches to read-only: everything you entered stays visible, searchable and exportable to CSV — forever, free. You just can't add new records until you subscribe again. We think that's the only honest way to run a paid finance app.

Common questions

How does the free trial work?

Every subscription starts with 14 days completely free — the full app, no limits. You'll get a reminder two days before it ends. No charge if you cancel before the trial is over.

How do I cancel?

Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Gilt → Cancel. Apple handles all billing; we never see your payment details.

Is Lifetime shared with my family?

Lifetime is a personal unlock for your Apple ID. For a shared household, the Family plan is the right fit.
